Mount and Shardblade is bringing Brandon Sanderson's Stormlight Archive to Mount and Blade II Bannerlord!

Current Bannerlord version: e1.5.3

* Brandon Sanderson (https://www.brandonsanderson.com/) for the Stormlight Archives, the book series that this mod is based off of.
* Taleworlds (https://www.taleworlds.com/) for developing Mount and Blade II Bannerlord.
* The Modding team making Mount and Shardblade.
